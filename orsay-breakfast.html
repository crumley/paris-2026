⏱️ Thursday timing: the Musée d'Orsay opens 9:30am (it's closed Mondays) and runs late on Thursday. To eat inside you need a ticket, so the play is a street/door breakfast first, then in. Everything on this map is open by 10am - most from 7-7:30am.

📍 One unpinned option: Café Blanc sits directly across from the Orsay entrance and is a popular pre-museum breakfast - but I couldn't verify its exact address to place a reliable pin, so treat it as a doorstep walk-up and confirm on the day.

Quick read: for a proper sit-down right at the door, La Frégate (Seine-side, from 7am). For the occasion breakfast, Les Deux Magots (7:30am, ~10 min into Saint-Germain). For coffee-forward, Cuppa (tiny, right behind the museum) or Coutume (best in the 7th). Carette's viral chocolat chaud is lovely but it's a Trocadéro detour, not an Orsay one - save it for an Eiffel morning.
