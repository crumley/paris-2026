⏱️ Morning vs. crêperie: real crêperies (the galette de sarrasin kind) open at lunch, ~noon - so an actual morning buckwheat crêpe isn't a thing here. Buckwheat opens at 12:00. For a bite before the exhibit, go to a dedicated gluten-free bakery (Copains / Wild & The Moon), then save the galette for lunch after.

🌾 GF note: buckwheat/sarrasin galettes are naturally wheat-free, but a working crêperie also flips wheat-flour sweet crêpes on the same range - so it's "naturally wheat-free," not certified GF. Strict celiac? Stick to the 100% dedicated GF bakeries. At Buckwheat, ask for a clean crêpe maker: "une crêpière propre, sans gluten - je suis cœliaque."
