🍷 Paris · Dinner — Food, Wine & a Terrace

Dinner spots chosen for atmosphere and people-watching, an outdoor table where possible, and genuinely great food with a proper classic French wine list — real cellars and sommellerie, not the natural-wine scene. No tourist landmarks; these are the chef- and cellar-led places Parisians actually book. Bonus points for a short hop from the Palais Royal hotel. Tap a pin or a card for more.

🍇 How this is sorted: the first group is walkable from 4 Rue de Valois (the hotel) — classic rooms in the 1st & 2nd. The second group is a short métro ride into the 11th, the 6th and the Marais, where the great bistros and the serious classic French cellars are — livelier and more local than the center. A green 🌿 TERRACE tag marks the ones with proper outdoor seating (you said you'd rather be outside). Everything here takes reservations — book the terrace tables and the small classics well ahead.

If you only pick one: for outside + atmosphere + serious food near the hotel, Restaurant du Palais Royal (garden terrace) is the splurge and Willis the easy wine-bar move. For the definitive classic-bistro night, La Poule au Pot near the hotel or Bistrot Paul Bert in the 11th. For the wine list itself, Vantre's cellar or Josephine Chez Dumonet's great Bordeaux & Burgundy by the half-bottle. And Grand Cœur is the prettiest hidden-courtyard terrace of the lot.
