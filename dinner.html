🍇 How this is sorted: the first group is walkable from 4 Rue de Valois (the hotel). The second group is a short métro ride into the 11th and the Marais — Paris's real food-and-wine quarter, livelier and more local than the center. A green 🌿 TERRACE tag marks the ones with proper outdoor seating (you said you'd prefer to be outside). Most of the wine-led spots take reservations well ahead; Clamato is walk-in only.

If you only pick one: for outside + atmosphere + serious food near the hotel, Restaurant du Palais Royal (garden terrace) is the splurge and Willis the easy wine-bar move. For the full lively-Paris evening, the 11th is worth the métro — Clown Bar for the room and the natural wine, Bistrot Paul Bert for the classic bistro-with-a-terrace night, Clamato if you'd rather walk in than book. And Grand Cœur is the prettiest hidden courtyard terrace of the lot.
