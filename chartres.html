A possible rhythm for the 5 days: one Chartres day (cathedral + kayak on the Eure → dinner in the old town → the nightly illuminations), the Versailles bike tour (penciled Jul 17), one teen-adventure day (Espace Rambouillet or Thoiry), a slow château day (Maintenon), and a down day at the Orangerie (terrace, a cooked-in dinner, a cellar tasting that evening). Nothing locked — this is just to react to.

One practical note: many châteaux and sights stop admitting visitors about an hour before they close, so aim sightseeing for late morning or early afternoon, not the tail end of the day.
