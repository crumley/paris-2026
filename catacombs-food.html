🎟️ The setup: you enter at Place Denfert-Rochereau for the 2pm slot and surface ~45–60 min later at the exit, 21bis Ave René Coty, about a 5-minute walk south. So the before spots cluster around the entrance and the rue Daguerre market street; the after spots are by the exit. Gluten-free: Slow is the best bet, with Indiana Café at the entrance, and Kapunka (all-GF Thai) plus La Guinguette d'Angèle a few minutes further toward Montparnasse.

Quick plan: reserve Slow (gluten-free) or grab a table at I Grappoli (pizza) for ~12:30 so you're done before the 2pm slot; after the tour, Fourteen Café is basically at the exit for a coffee or a glass. Everything here is open on a Wednesday.
