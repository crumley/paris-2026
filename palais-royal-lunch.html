🚆 Check-in day (Tue Jul 21): arriving by train from Chartres around midday. The hotel opens straight onto the Palais Royal gardens, so the low-effort move is a quick or takeaway lunch eaten in the gardens. All the gluten-free spots below are dedicated (no cross-contact) and open on a Tuesday — mind that CoCoCo stops lunch at 3pm and La Guinguette is weekday-only takeaway.
Quick & casual Dedicated gluten-free Sit-down Our hotel
Quick read: for the fastest arrival lunch, Higuma (ramen, no reservation) or a La Guinguette picnic in the gardens. For a proper sit-down, Bistrot Vivienne in the covered gallery. For the gluten-free eaters, Judy, CoCoCo or a Copains baguette — all dedicated 100% gluten-free.
